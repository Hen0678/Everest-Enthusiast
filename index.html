The allure of Everest

Few places on Our planet conjure up the mystery and excitement of Mount Everest. As the highest point on Earth this is the pinnacle of high-altitude trekking, and over the past century she has attracted considerable attention and climbing attempts.


In 1953 Edmund Hillary and Tenzing Norgay became the first climbers to reach the summit of Mount Everest. Like all pioneers, their bavery and resilience set an example for many other adventurers in the following decades. The challenge of scaling Everest still attracts thousands of people from all walks of life who want to push themselves to the limit.
